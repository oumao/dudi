ABOUT US

Who We Are

Dudi Development Program is a national humanitarian, development, non-profit and non-governmental organization dedicated to improving lives of the communities through poverty alleviation, asset creation, innovations, resilience and sustainable development mechanisms in partnership with national and international partners in Kenya.

Vision

Promote innovative, sustainable and solution-driven integrated development initiatives that empower communities.

Mission

To initiate integrated development programs focused on environmental management, increased access to health services, improved food and nutrition security, and to empower communities to manage and sustain these initiatives.

Goal

To strengthen capacities within communities for sound management and optimized utilization of resource in order to improve socio-economic welfare of their residents.




OBJECTIVES

- 1 -

Promote safe, environment-friendly and conservative agricultural practices.

- 2 -

Promote Land Rehabilitation, Land Use Planning and Re-afforestation programs.

- 3 -

Establish safe water for all through WASH programs.

- 4 -

Enhance mental health management, wellbeing and management of chronic diseases affected and infected households.

- 5 -

Design, initiate and strengthen programs that can address mental and behavioral disorders in communities.

- 6 -

Design, initiate and strengthen programs that can address chronic illness in communities

- 7 -

Support orphans and vulnerable persons in communities.

- 8 -

Promote adult literacy, youth education and vocational skills.

- 9 -

Undertaking research for capacity building with the purpose of assessing, informing and influencing the after mentioned specific objectives and also the organizations’ mission and vision.

- 10 -

Harness asset creating through income generation, innovations and VSLA’s for sustainable, resilient and dynamic society.
